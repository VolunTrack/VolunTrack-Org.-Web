About Us

Welcome to Voluntrack Org.

At Voluntrack, we are committed to connecting volunteers with non-profit organizations through innovative technology, aiming to create significant impacts in community development. As a legally incorporated entity under the Canada Not-for-profit Corporations Act, we strive to foster positive changes within communities.

Our Vision:

We aim to enrich the educational experiences of high school students, especially those in grades 9 to 12, by providing hands-on experiences in web design, development, and marketing—key areas in today’s digital landscape. Our programs combine these technical skills with academic learning to create a well-rounded educational environment that promotes both practical skills and core values such as empathy and teamwork.

Our Commitment

Our work extends beyond education to improve community well-being. We facilitate effective collaborations between volunteers and non-profits, strengthening the fabric of community service. Our status as a non-profit organization underscores our dedication to societal advancement and enables us to broaden our impact.

Our Approach

Voluntrack operates as a dynamic platform that connects volunteers with meaningful opportunities and enhances the operational capabilities of non-profit organizations. We believe in the synergy that can be achieved when individuals and organizations come together for communal benefit.

Our Governance

Governed by a board of directors who serve without remuneration, we adhere to the highest standards of integrity and transparency. Our directors, committed to our cause, ensure that any profits or accretions are used solely for the advancement of our purposes.

Join Us

Be a part of Voluntrack’s journey. Whether you are a student seeking to gain real-world experience, a volunteer looking to make a difference, or a non-profit organization aiming for greater efficacy, Voluntrack is your partner in creating a stronger, more connected community.

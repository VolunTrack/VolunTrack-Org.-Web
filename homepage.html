At Voluntrack,

Our aim is to facilitate meaningful connections between volunteers and non-profit organizations through innovative technology. Our primary goal is to enhance the educational experiences of high school students and contribute to the well-being of our communities.

Our efforts extend beyond education to embrace community well-being and the foundational values of community service. By integrating technology with community service, we strive to foster values like empathy and teamwork.

We are excited to introduce the VolunTrack app - our newest tool designed to simplify the interaction between volunteers and non-profits. This app serves as a platform for individuals eager to contribute and for non-profits in search of committed volunteers.

Explore our site to learn more about our programs, volunteer opportunities, and how you can be a part of this exciting journey.

Together, we build a better community.
